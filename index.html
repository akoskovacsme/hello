About Me

#FF5656 means a series of numbers for you. But it can be a good color for a button. This is how I realize the world around me over 8 years.

I always preferred the community-based knowledge, that’s why I trained myself. My skills/perspective came from the digital world. But I never forget the oldest tools: paper and pen to sketch my ideas. I always looking for new challenges in my life/career. I’m curious that’s the motivation for me. That was the reason why I choosed UI/UX community when I decided to leave the print materials.
